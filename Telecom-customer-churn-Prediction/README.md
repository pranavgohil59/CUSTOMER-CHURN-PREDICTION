Customer Churn Prediction is a machine learning-based project developed to identify telecom customers who are likely to discontinue their services. The system analyzes important customer attributes such as SeniorCitizen status, Partner, Dependents, tenure, PhoneService, InternetService, Contract type, PaymentMethod, MonthlyCharges, and TotalCharges to detect patterns associated with customer attrition. By preprocessing and training predictive models on historical customer data, the project accurately forecasts churn behavior. This enables telecom companies to understand customer retention challenges, improve decision-making, design targeted loyalty programs, reduce revenue loss, and enhance customer satisfaction through proactive retention strategies.
